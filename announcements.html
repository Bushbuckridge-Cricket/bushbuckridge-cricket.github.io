16 January 2026 Weather Update
Bushbuckridge CC Matches Abandoned Due to Severe Weather
Due to unusually heavy rainfall and persistent wet conditions affecting the White River and surrounding Lowveld areas in Mpumalanga over the past two weeks, the scheduled league matches on 11 January (vs Valencia 3 CC) and 18 January (vs Malekutu CC) were abandoned without play. Several weather warning alerts were issued for Mpumalanga and Limpopo during this period, with widespread downpours, flooding reports, and overflowing rivers making the pitch waterlogged and unplayable on both match days. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
